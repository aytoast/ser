## hacking tracks
1. **fine-tuning (sponsored by weights & biases)**
   - **goal:** fine-tune a mistral model to perform a task that the base model struggles at.
   - **requirement:** upload the fine-tuned model and adapters to the official hugging face org.
   - **tools:** encouraged use of w&b models and weave for tracking and evaluation.

2. **anything goes**
   - **goal:** use any mistral model to create the best project.
